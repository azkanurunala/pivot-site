Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026.

Pivot is an offline puzzle game. It does not require an account, does not show ads, and does not track you across apps or websites. There is no advertising identifier and no App Tracking Transparency prompt.

What stays on your device

All gameplay — solved levels, earned stars, equipped ball skin, settings, and your daily-challenge result — is stored locally on your device only. We do not upload it anywhere.

Purchases

If you buy Pivot Pro or a cosmetic, the purchase is processed by Apple and validated through RevenueCat. They receive only the transaction needed to deliver and restore your purchase (App Functionality). We never see your payment details.

Game Center

If you use the optional leaderboards, Apple Game Center handles your player ID and display name to rank your scores (App Functionality). We never see your Apple ID, and you can disable Game Center in iOS Settings.

Children

Pivot is rated 4+ and contains no objectionable content, ads, or data collection beyond what is described above.
