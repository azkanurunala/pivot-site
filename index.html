How do I restore my purchase?

Open the Pivot Pro sheet and tap Restore purchase, or go to Settings → Purchases → Restore. You can also redeem a gift code under Settings → Redeem.

I lost my progress

Progress is stored on your device, so reinstalling or resetting clears it. Pivot Pro itself is restored through your Apple ID.
